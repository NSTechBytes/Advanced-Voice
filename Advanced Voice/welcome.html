Welcome to Advanced Voice Extension!

Thank you for installing the Advanced Voice Chrome Extension! This tool provides a seamless way to convert text on any web page into spoken audio, offering voice customization, playback controls, and more. Whether you're reading articles, learning languages, or just want an easier way to consume web content, Advanced Voice makes it simple.

Features

  • Read Selected Text: Highlight text on any web page and listen to it being read aloud with a single click.
  • Voice and Speed Customization: Choose from a variety of voices and adjust the playback speed to your preference.
  • Pause and Resume: Control the playback with pause and resume functionality directly from the context menu.
  • Save Audio: Save the spoken text as an audio file for offline use or sharing.
  • Dark Mode: Easily toggle between light and dark modes for a more comfortable reading experience, especially in low light.

Advanced Voice makes text-to-speech functionality more accessible and customizable. From adjusting voice preferences to saving audio, everything is just a few clicks away. Control your experience directly from your Chrome browser with ease.

To get started, click the "Get Started" button below. If you need help or have any questions, please refer to our support documentation or reach out to our support team. We hope Advanced Voice enhances your browsing experience!
